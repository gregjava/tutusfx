What are I-qoption’s Underlying Assets?

Underlying assets are all assets being traded on the market, such as stocks, indices, commodities and forex currency pairs. The constantly changing market value of those assets is the basis for binary options trading. i-qoptions offers a variety of underlying assets for trading. For the full list of assets please visit our Asset Table.
